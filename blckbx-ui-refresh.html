— Signature

The diamond does the work

One motif, borrowed straight from the bullet marker in design.md, carries three jobs across both tools: it marks the LLM's rationale on every Edit card, it flags stock status in the catalogue, and it opens the needs-review rule. It's the only ornament on either screen — everything else is type, rule, and whitespace.
