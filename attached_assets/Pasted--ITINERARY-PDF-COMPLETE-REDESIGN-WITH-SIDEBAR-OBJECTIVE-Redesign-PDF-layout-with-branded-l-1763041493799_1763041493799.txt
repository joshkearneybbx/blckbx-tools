## 7. TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

### Using @react-pdf/renderer

**Document Structure:**
```tsx
<Document>
  <Page size="A4" style={styles.page}>
    {/* Sidebar - fixed position */}
    <View style={styles.sidebar} fixed>
      {/* Logo at top */}
      <Image src={logoWhite} style={styles.logo} />
      
      {/* Trip title */}
      <Text style={styles.tripTitle}>{title}</Text>
      
      {/* Flexible spacer */}
      <View style={{ flex: 1 }} />
      
      {/* Assistant info at bottom */}
      <View style={styles.assistantSection}>
        <Text style={styles.sectionHeading}>Your Assistant</Text>
        <Text style={styles.assistantName}>{assistant.name}</Text>
        <Link src={`mailto:${assistant.email}`} style={styles.assistantEmail}>
          {assistant.email}
        </Link>
      </View>
      
      {/* Page number at very bottom */}
      <Text style={styles.pageNumber} render={({ pageNumber, totalPages }) => 
        `Page ${pageNumber} of ${totalPages}`
      } fixed />
    </View>
    
    {/* Main content area */}
    <View style={styles.mainContent}>
      {/* All sections here */}
    </View>
  </Page>
</Document>
```
