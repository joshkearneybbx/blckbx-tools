## KEY CHANGES:

1. **NEVER use conditional rendering** (`{field && <Component />}`) for transfer sections
2. **ALWAYS show the section**, just change the styling
3. **When empty**: Smaller padding, lighter border, show "—" or "No details"
4. **When filled**: Normal padding, darker border, show content

## VISUAL DIFFERENCE:

**With Content (Full Size):**
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│ Transfer to Airport             │
│ Private car service picks up    │
│ from hotel at 8:00 AM           │
└─────────────────────────────────┘

**Without Content (Compact):**
┌──────────────────────┐
│ Transfer to Airport  │
│ —                    │
└──────────────────────┘

This way users can see all the transfer sections are there, but empty ones don't take up much space.
